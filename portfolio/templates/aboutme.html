About Me

My name is Mike, I'm a 29-year-old Police Officer who has been working in law enforcement since December of 2019. While committed to this current role, I am actively pursuing a career change into software development, with a focus on becoming a junior software engineer. I am passionate about full-stack development and continue to expand my technical skills while balancing my busy personal and professional life. I enjoy spending time with my girlfriend and three kids, making the most of my days off to be with them.





In addition to my law enforcement duties, I also work on Saturdays as a commercial driver for Amazon Freight Partners. Despite this busy schedule, I dedicate my spare time to learning about the tech industry, immersing myself in programming languages, full-stack development, and cybersecurity. I am enrolled in several online courses, including Harvard's CS50, Codecademy, and Udemy, to further my education and to help sharpen my programming skills.



My journey into tech began with HTML/HTML5, which I learned after buying a book on the subject. My enjoyment for front-end development, particularly writing HTML and CSS, has only grown, and I am eager to continue building my knowledge in full-stack development. My determination to transition into the tech industry, paired with self-driven learning and practical experience, positions myself as a dedicated candidate ready to embrace a new challenge in software engineering.
